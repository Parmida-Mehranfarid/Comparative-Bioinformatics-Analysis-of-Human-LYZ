# Multiple Sequence Alignment

## Objective

Multiple sequence alignment was performed using Clustal Omega to compare the amino acid sequences of the human LYZ protein with homologous sequences from closely related primate species.

## Files

- alignment_results.png

## Results

The alignment shows a high level of sequence conservation among primate LYZ proteins. Conserved amino acid residues are indicated by asterisks (*), demonstrating the evolutionary stability of the lysozyme protein.

## Conclusion

The high sequence similarity suggests that the LYZ protein has maintained its biological function throughout primate evolution.
